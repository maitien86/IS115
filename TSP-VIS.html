Traveling Salesperson Problem

Interactive Algorithm Visualizations & Comparisons

Individual Algorithms

1st Greedy Algorithm

Nearest Neighbor heuristic. Builds a path step-by-step by always selecting the closest unvisited city from the last selected city.

Open Visualization

2nd Greedy Algorithm

Closest Insertion heuristic. Maintains a closed cycle and continuously inserts the unvisited city that results in the lowest overall distance increase.

Open Visualization

2-Opt Local Search

Edge swap improvement. Scans a random tour for crossing edges and untangles them by reversing sub-tours to reach a local optimum.

Open Visualization
